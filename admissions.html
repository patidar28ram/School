Sarthak Acadmey teaches students the competencies to become higher learners and employable citizens. We strive to not only teach our students but to drive them towards academic excellence. 

SCHOOL TIMINGS

Nursery: Monday to Friday – 7.30 a.m. – 11.30 am
Grade I to X: Monday to Friday – 7.30 a.m. – 2.00 p.m.

Second Saturday, Fourth and Fifth Saturday – 7.30 a.m. to 11.30 a.m. (Grades V to X) 
Pre-primary and Primary students have a holiday on all Saturdays.

Admission Enquiry for the session 2022-23

ADMISSION POLICY

Parents wishing to apply to Sarthak Acadmey should complete the following steps:

step 01

ARRANGE A VISIT TO THE SCHOOL BY SETTING AN APPOINTMENT WITH THE ADMISSIONS OFFICE.

step 02

REQUEST FOR REGISTRATION FORM EITHER FROM THE SCHOOL OFFICE OR CLICK ON "ENROLL NOW" TO FILL UP THE FORM.

step 03

SUBMIT FILLED FORM 

ELIGIBILITY

Nursery

  • Age limit for Pre-Nursery is 3 years as on September 30th of the year of admission.

Class-I

  • Age limit for Class I is 6 years as on September 30th of the year of admission.

DOCUMENTS FOR SUBMISSION

  • Filled up Registration Form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Transfer certificate, progress sheet of the last examination taken
  • Official copy of reports/marks card from previous school
  • Eight passport size photographs
